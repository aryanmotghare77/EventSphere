Frequently Asked Questions

What is an event management platform?

An event management platform, is a software solution that allows you to plan, execute, analyze, optimize and manage every aspect of your events.

With an event management software you can manage every aspect of your in-person, virtual and hybrid events, such as registrations, ticketing, on-site check-in, attendee engagement, analytics and so much more.

What aspects of my events can I manage using Eventtia?

Eventtia significantly enhances the digital experience of thousands of events by offering a comprehensive suite of features such as online registration and ticketing, on-site check-in and real time reporting.

With powerful marketing features like event pages and promotional emails, Eventtia’s event management platform can help you achieve your online registration and ticket sales goals.

Eventtia also offers a customizable event app for attendees to access event details, agenda, schedules, networking, matchmaking and meetings.

Can Eventtia's event management platform handle large-scale events?

Absolutely. Eventtia is used by some of the largest companies in the world, managing thousands of events globally on a daily basis.

Our event platform can efficiently handle a high volume of attendees and complex event logistics, ensuring smooth execution at any scale.

Additionally, Eventtia’s integration capabilities with CRM systems and marketing platforms simplify event management and data handling, making it a versatile solution for organizations of any size.

Since 2014, we’ve proudly served global corporations such as the Pernod Ricard group, Richemont group, Pierre Fabre group, Campari group, the world’s top sporting goods brand, and many more.

How is Eventtia different from other event management platforms?

At Eventtia, we pride ourselves on fostering strong and long-lasting partnerships with our clients.

That’s why leading companies such as Campari, Van Cleef & Arpels, Toyota, Nestle, Bioderma, BBVA, Pernod Ricard, and many others have partnered with Eventtia, to help them digitize and scale their events throughout the years.

Unlike other event management softwares, Eventtia offers a fully customizable and evolutive solution tailored to meet your unique needs.

With Eventtia, you have the freedom to execute your events precisely the way you envision, in total respect of your brand identity and your brand guidelines.

Can Eventtia be integrated with other systems through API?

Yes, some of the world’s top brands have been leveraging Eventtia’s event management API for many years, to seamlessly exchange data between Eventtia and their existing IT systems for customer management (CRM), databases and datalakes, accounting platforms, marketing automation platforms, and others.

Some have even developed their own event platforms on top of Eventtia’s API.

For more information on Eventtia’s API services, please visit our API documentation.
